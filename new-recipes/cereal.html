Cereal

Description:

This a recipe for making cereal. Nothing groundbreaking, just simple and concise; and perhaps a tip or two for more variety.

Ingredients:

  • A bowl and spoon
  • Your favorite cereal
  • Milk
  • Fruits (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Pour your favorite cereal brand into an empty bowl. Don't pour too much!
    2. If you like, you can add some fruits, too! Banana slices and berries work best here.
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Eat cold right away or microwave. Enjoy!
